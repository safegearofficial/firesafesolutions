Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do in case of a wildfire near my area?

Follow evacuation orders immediately, prepare an emergency kit, and stay informed via trusted sources like CAL FIRE.

How can I help wildfire victims?

Donate to organizations like Red Cross or local fire departments. Avoid unsolicited physical donations unless requested.
