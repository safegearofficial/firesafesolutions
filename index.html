Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do during a wildfire evacuation?

Follow evacuation orders immediately. Pack an emergency kit, including important documents, medications, and essentials.

How can I protect my home from wildfires?

Clear flammable debris, maintain defensible space, and use fire-resistant materials for roofing and siding.

Where can I report a wildfire?

Call 911 or contact your local fire department if you spot a wildfire.
