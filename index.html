FAQ

What should I do if a wildfire is approaching?

Evacuate immediately if ordered by local authorities. Prepare an emergency kit and follow a planned evacuation route.

How can I stay updated?

Use apps and websites like Cal Fire, local news stations, or emergency alerts to get live updates.

Can I help firefighters?

Yes, consider donating to firefighter relief funds or volunteering at community support organizations.

Preparation Tips

  • Create an emergency kit with essentials like water, food, and first-aid supplies.
  • Have an evacuation plan and practice it with your family.
  • Maintain a defensible space around your property by clearing flammable debris.
  • Keep important documents in a fireproof safe or digitally backed up.
  • Stay informed via official channels and be ready to leave when instructed.
